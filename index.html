Welcome to Ranikhet!

Nestled in the Kumaon hills, Ranikhet is a paradise for Kumaoni & Garhwali communities and travelers alike. Famous for its lush pine forests, panoramic Himalayan views, ancient temples, and peaceful ambiance, Ranikhet is a perfect getaway to explore the best of Uttarakhand's traditions, culture, and nature.

Top Places to Visit

Jhula Devi Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Durga, this 8th-century temple is famous for its countless bells and spiritual vibes.

Chaubatia Gardens

Spread over 600 acres, these gardens are known for apple, peach, plum, and apricot orchards with mesmerizing Himalayan views.

Majkhali

A beautiful hamlet with stunning views of the snow-capped Trishul peaks, perfect for a peaceful retreat.

Bhalu Dam

This small artificial lake is ideal for picnics and birdwatching, surrounded by dense forests.

Kumaon Regimental Centre Museum

Discover the history and valor of the Kumaon Regiment with fascinating exhibits and stories.

Haida Khan Babaji Temple

Set atop a hill, this peaceful temple offers panoramic views and serene surroundings.

Kumaoni & Garhwali Culture

The vibrant culture of Kumaon & Garhwal is reflected in its folk music, traditional dances like Chholiya and Jhora, fairs, and festivals like Harela, Phool Dei, and Ghee Sankranti. Local cuisine such as Aloo Ke Gutke, Bhatt Ki Churkani, and Bal Mithai is a must-try!

  • Languages: Kumaoni, Garhwali, Hindi
  • Famous Festivals: Harela, Nanda Devi Mela, Kandali Festival
  • Handicrafts: Woolen shawls, Aipan art, Ringaal crafts
